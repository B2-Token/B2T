About

B2T is a fully deflationary utility token on BNB Smart Chain, inspired by the B-2 Spirit. With 21M fixed supply, 1% burn per transfer, and no tax, it offers a stealthy and community-driven ecosystem.

Tokenomics

💎 Total Supply: 21,000,000 B2T.
🔥 Burn: 1% on every transfer (deflationary).
🚫 Tax: 0% — completely tax-free.

📊 Initial Allocation:
• 🛡️ Founder Reserve: 5% (1,050,000 B2T)
• 🚀 Vault (Growth & Marketing): 10% (2,100,000 B2T)
• 🌐 Deployer (Community & Liquidity): 85% (17,850,000 B2T)
